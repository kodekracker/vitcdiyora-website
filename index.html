about us

Vivekanand Industrial Training Centre has been established in 2012 at Miyanpur Deora , Islamnagar, in the district Badaun .Institute is started with single trade Electrician and going to achieve new heights soon.

With a view to meet the huge demand of technically qualified persons, Vivekanand Industrial Training Centre, Badaun has been setup. The institute has its own modern building surrounded by the natural environment with facilities of workshop, administrative wing, store, library and play ground.

It gives me a great pleasure to introduce to you to Vivekanand Industrial Training Centre, Badaun, under the aegis of ITI technical Industrial Education System which will help the lives of thousands of Aspirants.
